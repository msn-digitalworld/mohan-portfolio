## üìù How to Document Your Projects

### For Each Project, Include:

1. **Problem Statement**
   - What was wrong with the old design?
   - What were the user pain points?

2. **Process**
   - How did you use AI tools?
   - What was your design process?
   - Show wireframes, mockups, iterations

3. **Before & After**
   - Side-by-side comparison
   - Highlight key improvements
   - Show metrics/improvements

4. **Design System**
   - Color palette
   - Typography
   - Component library
   - Spacing system

5. **Tools Used**
   - Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch
   - AI tools (Figma AI, DALL¬∑E, Midjourney)
   - Prototyping tools
